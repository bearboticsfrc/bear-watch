How to Disable Randomized MAC Addresses

For iOS

To disable randomized MAC addresses on an iOS, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Settings app on your device.
  2. Go to Wi-Fi settings.
  3. Tap the info (i) next to the current network you are connected to (MITEE Explorers)
  4. Toggle off the Private Address switch.
  5. Copy the new MAC address and paste it on the sign up page.

For Android

To disable randomized MAC addresses on an Android device, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Settings app on your device.
  2. Go to Network & Internet (or Connections depending on your device).
  3. Tap Wi-Fi to view available networks.
  4. Tap the current network you are connected to (MITEE Explorers).
  5. Tap Advanced or the gear icon next to the network.
  6. Find and select MAC Address Type or MAC Address.
  7. Change the setting from Randomized to Phone's MAC or Use Device MAC.
  8. Go back to the main settings menu.
  9. Go to About Phone at the bottom.
  10. Go to Status information.
  11. Tap and hold on Phone Wi-Fi MAC Address and copy.
  12. Paste the MAC Address on the sign up page.

For Windows

To disable randomized MAC addresses on a Windows device, follow these steps:

  1. Open Settings by pressing Windows + I.
  2. Go to Network & Internet.
  3. Select Wi-Fi and click on the network you are connected to or want to connect to.
  4. Click on Properties.
  5. Under Hardware properties, find the Network profile section.
  6. Set the Random hardware addresses toggle to Off.
  7. Copy the new MAC address and paste it on the sign up page.

For macOS

To disable randomized MAC addresses on a macOS device, follow these steps:

  1. macOS does not support randomized MAC addresses, nothing to do!
  2. Copy your MAC address and paste it on the sign up page.
